## Summary

This phase involved creating the project charter to define the mission and bounds of the project early on. The charter explicitly focuses on offline-first, audio-based learning specifically designed for blind and low-vision learners. Non-goals were clarified to avoid feature creep into gamification, heavy social aspects, or unnecessary cloud dependencies for the MVP.

## Decisions made

- Explicitly declared AI tutors and Cloud Sync as optional enhancements that shouldn't impact the core MVP experience offline.
- Explicitly stated soundscapes are asset-driven (JSON/WAV) rather than dynamically generated by AI.

## Risks

- Defining clear lines around the AI Tutor might become difficult if the distinction isn't carefully maintained by developers.

## Open questions
